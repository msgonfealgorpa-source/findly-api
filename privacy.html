Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Welcome to Findly. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle information when you use our shopping assistant.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our services, including:

  • Account Information: If you sign up, we store your email and user ID via Firebase Authentication.
  • Search Data: We may process your search queries to fetch results from third-party APIs like Alibaba.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide personalized search results and watchlists.
  • Ensure account security through Firebase.
  • Improve the performance of our AI shopping assistant.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate with trusted partners:

  • Google Firebase: For user authentication and database security.
  • Alibaba Open Platform: To provide real-time product data and pricing.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security rules (Cloud Firestore Security Rules) to ensure that only you can access your private data.
